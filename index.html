Brand Bio

URelief was founded to make emotional support simple, affordable, and anonymous. We believe everyone deserves a safe space to speak—whether to vent, ask for an opinion, or release stress. Our listeners are trained in active listening and empathy; they are not therapists, but compassionate humans ready to hear you.

Mission: To provide instant, judgment-free listening to anyone who needs it.
Vision: A world where no one has to carry stress alone.
