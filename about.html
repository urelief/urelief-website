Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

➤ What is URelief?

URelief is a support platform that helps people reduce stress, gain clarity, and receive emotional guidance whenever needed.

➤ How does URelief help me?

We listen to your problems and provide simple, practical solutions. You can talk about anything—personal, academic, emotional, or professional.

➤ Is my information private?

Yes. Everything you share stays completely confidential and secure.

➤ Who can use URelief?

Anyone dealing with stress, confusion, anxiety, relationship issues, academic pressure, or emotional challenges.

➤ How can I book a session?

You can book directly through the website using the “Book Now” section on the home page.

➤ Is this a therapy service?

No, URelief is a guidance and support platform—not a clinical medical service.
